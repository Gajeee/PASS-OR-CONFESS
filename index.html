How To Play

1. The Setup

Add players, set the timer, and pick a difficulty.

2. Your Turn

Read the card. You have two choices.

Confess:

Answer the question truthfully. The group votes. If they believe you, +2 Points. If not, -2 Points.

Pass:

Don't want to answer? You lose 1 Point and must do a random penalty.

3. Winning

The game ends when rounds run out. Highest score wins!
